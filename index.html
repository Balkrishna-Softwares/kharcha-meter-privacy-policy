📱 About Kharcha Meter

Welcome to Kharcha Meter – your simple and smart daily expense tracking app.

Managing expenses can be tricky, but Kharcha Meter makes it easy, fast, and stress-free. The app is designed for anyone who wants to record daily kharcha (expenses) in the most convenient way.

✨ What Kharcha Meter Provides

  • 📝 Quick Expense Entry – Add your daily expenses in just a few taps.
  • 📊 Local Storage Only – All your data is saved securely on your device only. No signup, no internet required for core features, and no expense data sharing.
  • 🔍 Category-based Tracking – Organize your expenses into categories like Food, Travel, Shopping, and more.
  • 🎯 Simple & User-Friendly – Clean design with easy navigation for a smooth experience.
  • 🔒 Privacy First – Since everything is stored locally, your expense data remains private and accessible only on your device.

🚀 App Reliability & Improvements

  • ⚙️ App Stability Monitoring – The app uses crash monitoring tools to detect technical issues and improve stability.
  • 📈 Anonymous Usage Insights – Limited anonymous usage analytics may be collected to help improve the app experience. No personal or financial data is collected.
  • 🔄 Mandatory Updates – Occasionally the app may require an update from the Google Play Store to ensure security, stability, and compatibility.
  • ✨ Improved Loading Experience – Smooth loading indicators provide a better user experience when viewing data.

💡 Why Choose Kharcha Meter?

  • Perfect for anyone who wants a lightweight, offline expense manager.
  • Helps you stay aware of your daily spending habits.
  • No ads, no distractions – just pure tracking made easy.
  • Designed with privacy and simplicity in mind.

Kharcha Meter is here to help you stay in control of your daily kharcha with simplicity and privacy.
