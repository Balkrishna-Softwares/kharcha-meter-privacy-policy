📄 Privacy Policy – Kharcha Meter

Effective Date: 10/03/2026

Kharcha Meter ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Collection: Kharcha Meter does not require registration or login. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment details, or financial information.
  • Local Data Storage: All expense records you add in the App are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data and it is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Anonymous Technical Information: The App may collect limited anonymous technical information such as app version, device type, operating system version, and general usage events to help improve app stability and performance. This information does not include any personal or financial data.

2. How Your Data is Used

All the expense data you enter is used solely for your personal tracking and remains on your device. Since no financial or personal data is sent to our servers, we do not share, sell, or disclose your expense information to any third parties.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in the local database of your device. We recommend regular device backups to avoid accidental loss of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses limited third-party services provided by Google Firebase to improve app reliability and performance.

  • Firebase Analytics: Used to understand general feature usage such as screen views and interactions. This data is collected anonymously and does not include expense details, category names, or personal information.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to detect app crashes and technical issues to improve app stability. Crash reports may include device model, operating system version, and technical diagnostic information.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Used to manage application configuration such as required app updates and feature settings.

These services may collect anonymous device and technical information according to Google's privacy policies. For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages as we do not collect any personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
